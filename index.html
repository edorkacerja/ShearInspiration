    ~ Print out a (wedding contract) from this site & email it when completed
    ~ Print out (Wedding day checklist) so your girls have all of the important (to do & not to do’s)
    ~ once you are positive of your head count, fill out ALL fields on contract
    & email contract to Shearinspiration@gmail.com
    *your deposit is applied toward your final bill
    ~ you may Bring it in person (OR) email your completed contract to:
    Shearinspiration@gmail.com
    ~ You will be confirmed by Email when your contract is received.
    THEN YOUR RESERVATION WILL BE SECURE

    *Please note
    if all fields are not filled in, your contract is not complete

    Final bill is due, in full, by one payment source, 7 days prior to Event date by
    Check, Cash, master card or Visa.

    & enjoying your friends & family on your special day!
    gratuity can be added into your final bill for your convenience
    ~$100 on location fee is added for onsite services per artist/stylist
    A Valid Credit Card Must be kept on file to hold any
    group reservations *This CC Will Be The Default
    Payment for total bill if unpaid by another SINGLE
    method (cash or check)

    Entire reservation is to be paid in full by one payment
    source, we do not bill each person individually for
    Group Bookings.

    *all billing is final upon signing of contract.
